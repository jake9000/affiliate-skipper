Automatically skip a video when an affiliate sticker is in play on youtube. 

You can be sure that the entire video from end to end is just an ad except the affiliate is too cheap to actually pay into adsense, so instead they pollute the content stream with their advertisements. 

**Installation:**

1. Aquire a userscript runner such as Tampermonkey or Violentmonkey or whichever new monkey is in vogue this week. 

2. [Click here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jake9000/affiliate-skipper/refs/heads/main/youtube-affiliate-skipper.user.js). Your script manager should present you with an install button.
